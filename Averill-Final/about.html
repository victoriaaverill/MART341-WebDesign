Meet Victoria!

Biography

Hi! My name is Victoria and I live in a small town in Montana. Ever since I sparked an interest for photography in high school, I have been eager to make something out of it. I bought my first camera my sophomore year of college and although there was a slight learning curve, I did my best to hit the ground running. I practice any chance I get and have done everything from weddings to senior portraits to website photos for business. I love making things people look at or walk past every day look like gold. I have started using that to help small businesses develop quality photos that make people stop and become interested in what they see. I am working on a degree in Media Arts and plan to then venture into marketing. I am always looking for experience and hope to make Victo Media into something bigger than just me.
