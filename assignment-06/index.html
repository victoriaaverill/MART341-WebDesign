Easy Egg Roll Mozarella Sticks!

Ingredients (for 10 sticks)

  • 5 egg roll wrappers
  • 10 string cheese sticks
  • Water
  • Oil (for frying)
  • Marinara sauce or ranch (optional)

Directions

  1. Slice the egg roll wrappers in half.
  2. Place one stick of string cheese on the edge of the egg roll wrapper, then fold the sides of the wrapper over the ends of the cheese.
  3. By dipping your finger in some water, wet the other edge of the wrapper, then roll it up tightly, pressing the edge to make sure it is sealed.
  4. Repeat with the remaining wrappers and cheese.
  5. Heat oil in a pot
  6. Fry preppared sticks until golden brown, then drain on a paper towel
  7. Optional. Serve with marinara
  8. Enjoy!
